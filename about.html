About Akeva Technologies

Our Story

Akeva Technologies was founded by a team of cybersecurity and AI governance experts who recognized the growing need for integrated solutions that address both security and compliance challenges in the digital age.

With years of combined experience in information security, risk management, and artificial intelligence, our team has developed a comprehensive approach to helping organizations navigate the increasingly complex landscape of digital security and AI governance.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help organizations navigate the complexities of digital security and artificial intelligence management, enabling them to operate confidently while adhering to the highest standards of governance and risk management.

We believe that effective security and governance practices should be integrated into the organizational culture, not treated as separate functions. By taking this approach, we help our clients stay ahead of regulatory changes, mitigate risks, and enhance their resilience against cyber threats.

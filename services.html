Our Services

Akeva Technologies provides comprehensive cybersecurity and AI governance services, specializing in ensuring compliance with industry frameworks and regulations. Our services are designed to streamline the integration of security and governance practices into your organizational culture.

ISO 27001 Cybersecurity Services

Gap Analysis & Readiness Assessment

Evaluate your current security posture against ISO 27001 requirements to identify gaps and develop a roadmap for compliance.

ISMS Implementation

Design and implement an Information Security Management System tailored to your organization's specific needs and risk profile.

Risk Assessment & Treatment

Identify, analyze, and develop treatment plans for information security risks in accordance with ISO 27001 requirements.

Documentation Development

Create comprehensive policies, procedures, and controls documentation required for ISO 27001 certification.

Internal Auditing

Conduct thorough internal audits to ensure your ISMS meets ISO 27001 requirements and identify areas for improvement.

Certification Support

Guide your organization through the certification process, including preparation for external audits and addressing findings.

ISO 42001 AI Governance Services

AI Risk Assessment

Identify and evaluate risks associated with your AI systems, including ethical, legal, and operational considerations.

AI Management System Implementation

Design and implement an AI Management System that aligns with ISO 42001 requirements and your organizational objectives.

AI Ethics Framework Development

Create comprehensive frameworks for ensuring ethical AI development, deployment, and operation within your organization.

AI Compliance Monitoring

Establish processes for ongoing monitoring of AI systems to ensure continued compliance with regulatory requirements.

AI Governance Training

Provide training for staff at all levels on AI governance principles, practices, and responsibilities.

AI Certification Readiness

Prepare your organization for ISO 42001 certification through gap analysis, documentation development, and audit preparation.

Management Systems Integration

Integrated Management System Design

Design comprehensive management systems that integrate cybersecurity, AI governance, and other compliance requirements.

Process Optimization

Streamline processes to eliminate redundancies and enhance efficiency across multiple management systems.

Unified Documentation Framework

Develop a cohesive documentation framework that addresses requirements across multiple standards and regulations.

Integrated Audit Programs

Establish audit programs that efficiently assess compliance with multiple standards simultaneously.
